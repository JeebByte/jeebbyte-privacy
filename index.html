JeebByte — Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-07-15 · App: JeebByte (Learn Linux & the command line)

Short version: JeebByte does not collect any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. The app does not send your information anywhere, because it has no server to send it to. Everything you do stays on your phone.

Data we collect

None. JeebByte has no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, and no advertising ID. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, files, or any usage or device information. We do not have a server that receives data from the app.

Data stored on your device

The app saves a few things locally, on your phone only, so it can work:

  • Your learning progress (which lessons you finished, XP, streak, energy).
  • Your settings (lesson language, and the display name you may optionally type in Settings).

This never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or anyone else. The optional name is used only to address you inside lessons. Uninstalling the app deletes all of it.

Permissions

The app requests internet access only so that optional features (such as opening an external link you tap) can work, and notification permission only to show a learning reminder if you allow it. No permission is used to collect or transmit personal data.

Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data — because we do not collect any.

Third parties

JeebByte does not include advertising networks or analytics services. If you tap a link that opens an external website (for example, to download Termux), that website's own privacy policy applies to your visit there.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, export, correct, or delete on our side. All data lives on your device and is under your control: clear the app's storage or uninstall it to remove it completely. You may still contact us with any privacy question.

Children

JeebByte is a general-audience educational app and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected, no child's data is collected.

Contact

JeebByte — jeebbyte@gmail.com

If a future version of JeebByte ever adds accounts, analytics, or any data collection, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the Play Store Data Safety section will be updated in the same release.
